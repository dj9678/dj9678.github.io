Google Ecommerce Analysis

By Dongsuk Jeong | May 2023 - July 2023

Purpose

• The goal of this project is to analyze the google ecommerce dataset and find out the various insights such as website performance and user behaviors

Methodology


Data Collection

• Collect the google ecommerce big query sample dataset through API using Python
• Unnested the original json data format and created the pandas data frame
• Changed the columns name and dropped unnecessary variables for efficient memory usage
• Exported data frames and stored them to PostgreSQL server

User Behavior Analysis

• Daily user count, daily visit counts, average daily visit counts
• Daily Active User, Weekly Active User, Monthly Active User,and Stickiness Metric
• Weekly User Session Visit Counts Distribution
• Average Times, Maximum Times, Minimum Times, and Quartile Percentile for users to connect to their second session after their first
• User Visit Counts by Countries

Website Performance Analysis

• Conversion Rate by Chennel
• Total User Count and Ratio by devices
• Weekly User Count and Ratio by devices
• Top 5 Most Hit Pages
• Daily Page Hit Counts during Past 30 days and the average daily page hit count over 30 daysTop 5 Most Hit Pages
• Average Time Spent per Page over past 30 days
• Past 7 days Conversion Rate
• Past 30 Days Bounce Rate per Page

• Past 30 Days Bounce Rate by Channel
• Past 30 Days Bounce Rate of Channel per Pages
• Past 30 Days Exit Rate per Page
• Conversion Funnel for Past 30 Days
• Conversion Funnel by Channel for Past 30 Days

Conclusion

• Average time for User to connect second session after first is approximately 14 mintutes
• The countires that has the most user visit counts is United States, then India
• Referral chennel shows the the highest conversion rate and affiliate showed the lowest conversion rate
• Among devices, desktop showed the most user visit and tablet showed the least
• The number of daily page hits drops significantly after December 21st in 2016
• Users tend to spend relatively little time on homepage and basket page compared to products pages
• On December 25th, conversion rate reaches to 1.48 and drops significantly after 25th
• Bounce rate tend to peak at the search, store, and electronics pages considering the number of user counts
• Among chennel group, social showed the highest bounce rate and referral shows the least
• Homepage and youtube page have the highest exit rate compared to other pages
• Only 1.5 Percent of users complete to purchase the products in conversion funnel
• Approximately, 86 percent of users are losted from homepage to product list pages
